Our Team

Deukyoung Cha, PT, DPT

Founder & Clinical Director
Cha Physical Therapy

Credentials:

  • Doctor of Physical Therapy, Stony Brook University
  • Member, American Physical Therapy Association (since 2003)
  • APTA Credentialed Clinical Instructor

For over 25 years, Dr. Cha has devoted his life to mastering the art and science of physical therapy, transforming his practice into a pursuit of excellence that honors both his patients and his craft. Armed with his Doctor of Physical Therapy degree from Stony Brook University, he approaches each patient encounter not merely as a treatment session, but as a profound opportunity to deliver the highest level of care while continuously deepening his understanding of the human body's infinite complexity.

Dr. Cha's philosophy centers on seeing the human body as a micro-universe—an intricate system where every movement, breath, and posture connects to create optimal health. His methodical approach begins with normalizing movement as the foundation, then progresses to optimizing breathing and postural alignment, ensuring that healing occurs at the deepest level possible.

This dedication to mastery extends beyond the clinic walls. As an avid hiker, climber, and cyclist, and through his disciplined practice of the traditional Korean martial arts of Kuksun-Do and Gicheon, Dr. Cha embodies the very principles of movement integrity and balance that he teaches his patients. His commitment to family as a devoted husband and father reflects the same values of care, dedication, and honor that define his approach to healing—making him not just a practitioner, but a living example of the holistic wellness he helps others achieve.

Mansoo Kim, PT, DPT, L.Ac.

Clinical Director
Cha Physical Therapy P.C. II

Credentials:

  • Doctor of Physical Therapy, Dominican College
  • Licensed Acupuncturist, New York State
  • Graduate, Yong-In University, South Korea

Dr. Mansoo Kim embodies the rare mastery that comes from bridging two worlds of healing. As Clinical Director of Cha Physical Therapy P.C. II since 2019, he has cultivated over 15 years of clinical excellence, earning his credentials from both Yong-In University in South Korea and Dominican College in New York. His unique dual-licensure in physical therapy and acupuncture represents more than professional achievement—it reflects a profound commitment to honoring the wisdom of both Eastern and Western healing traditions.

Dr. Kim's approach transcends conventional treatment boundaries, seamlessly weaving advanced manual therapy and orthopedic rehabilitation with time-honored practices including cupping, tuina, and trigger point acupuncture. This integration creates a therapeutic experience that addresses not just symptoms, but the complete person. His expertise in neuromuscular and orthopedic conditions—from stroke recovery and surgical rehabilitation to sports injuries—is distinguished by his ability to see each patient through multiple healing lenses, crafting individualized treatments that honor both evidence-based methodology and Eastern wisdom.

In Dr. Kim's hands, physical therapy becomes an art form where Eastern philosophy meets Western precision, creating a healing environment where patients experience the profound benefits of truly integrated care. His dedication to this holistic vision makes him not just a clinician, but a bridge between healing traditions, offering his patients the best of both worlds in their journey toward optimal health.

Chongmin Park, DDS

TMJ Botox/Fillers Specialist

Credentials:

  • DDS, University at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine (2002)
  • AEGD Residency, University at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine
  • Aesthetic Dentistry Course, University at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine
  • Invisalign Premier Provider

With a captivating blend of precision and artistry, Dr. Chongmin Park brings over two decades of expertise to Cha Physical Therapy, specializing in Botox and dermal fillers for temporomandibular joint (TMJ) disorders. A 2002 graduate of the University at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine, Dr. Park honed his skills through a rigorous one-year Advanced Education in General Dentistry (AEGD) residency at the same institution, followed by an intensive one-year aesthetic dentistry course that deepened his mastery of facial harmony and functional restoration.

As an Invisalign Premier Provider, Dr. Park excels in transformative treatments that not only alleviate TMJ-related pain, tension, and dysfunction but also enhance natural beauty with subtle, confidence-boosting results. His approach integrates advanced injectable techniques to relax overactive muscles, reduce inflammation, and restore balanced jaw mechanics—empowering patients to reclaim effortless smiles and pain-free movement.

Drawing from a foundation in comprehensive dental care, he tailors each session to individual needs, blending science with an artistic eye for aesthetics that leaves clients feeling revitalized and radiant. Dr. Park's passion for innovation stems from his commitment to holistic well-being, viewing the face as a canvas where form meets function. Whether addressing chronic headaches, jaw clicking, or asymmetry, his gentle, expert touch delivers lasting relief and renewed vitality.
