Pain Management

What Is Pain Management?

Pain management involves using a variety of therapeutic techniques to address acute or chronic pain caused by injury, illness, or musculoskeletal conditions. The goal is to control pain levels, restore mobility, and empower you with tools to manage discomfort independently.

Common conditions we treat:

  • Chronic back and neck pain
  • Arthritis and joint pain
  • Post-surgical pain and recovery
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Sports injuries and overuse pain
  • Neuropathic pain and nerve irritation
  • Headaches and migraines related to musculoskeletal issues

How We Treat It

At Cha Physical Therapy, we create personalized pain management programs based on your diagnosis, symptoms, and lifestyle. Our approach combines hands-on techniques with therapeutic exercises and education to promote healing and prevent recurrence.

Our therapy may include:

  • Manual Therapy – Hands-on techniques to reduce muscle tension and improve joint mobility
  • Therapeutic Exercises – Increase strength, flexibility, and endurance tailored to your condition
  • Modalities for Symptom Relief – Ultrasound, TENS, or heat/cold therapy to manage pain
  • Posture and Body Mechanics Training – Minimize pain triggers and prevent future injuries
  • Relaxation and Breathing Techniques – Decrease pain-related stress and promote healing
  • Patient Education – Learn about pain neuroscience and self-management strategies for long-term success

Each plan is tailored to your goals, ensuring the most effective path to lasting relief.

The primary goal is to reduce pain and restore function so you can return to the activities you love. Our holistic approach addresses both the physical and emotional aspects of pain.
