Orthopedic Manual Therapy

What Is Orthopedic Manual Therapy?

Orthopedic manual therapy is a specialized form of physical therapy that uses hands-on techniques to evaluate, treat, and rehabilitate musculoskeletal conditions. Our therapists use targeted manual techniques to restore mobility, reduce pain, and improve function in your joints, muscles, and connective tissues.

Orthopedic manual therapy can help you with:

  • Joint pain and stiffness
  • Muscle strains and tension
  • Limited range of motion
  • Post-surgical rehabilitation
  • Sports injuries
  • Chronic pain conditions
  • Ligament and tendon injuries
  • Postural dysfunction

Common causes requiring orthopedic manual therapy include:

  • Repetitive strain or overuse
  • Acute injuries or trauma
  • Poor ergonomics or posture
  • Age-related wear and tear
  • Immobility or deconditioning

How We Treat It

At Cha Physical Therapy, we provide comprehensive orthopedic manual therapy tailored to your specific condition and goals. Our licensed therapists use evidence-based techniques to restore normal tissue function, reduce inflammation, and enhance your body's healing capacity.

Your personalized orthopedic manual therapy treatment may include:

  • Joint Mobilizations & Manipulations – Restoring normal joint movement and alignment
  • Soft Tissue Mobilization – Treating muscle tension, trigger points, and fascial restrictions
  • Myofascial Release Techniques – Releasing tension in the connective tissue surrounding muscles
  • Therapeutic Massage – Improving circulation and reducing muscle tightness
  • Stretching and Flexibility Training – Restoring normal range of motion
  • Strengthening Exercises – Building muscle stability and preventing re-injury
  • Postural Education – Improving alignment to prevent future problems
  • Home Exercise Program – Reinforcing progress and maintaining gains between sessions

We focus on understanding the root cause of your pain or dysfunction, not just treating symptoms. Our hands-on approach combined with targeted exercises helps you return to your normal activities with confidence.

Frequently Asked Questions about Orthopedic Manual Therapy:

Orthopedic manual therapy is a hands-on approach to treating musculoskeletal injuries and conditions. Our trained physical therapists use their hands to apply specific techniques directly to your muscles, joints, and tissues. These techniques are grounded in anatomy and biomechanics, designed to reduce pain, improve motion, and restore normal function. It's a non-invasive alternative to surgery for many conditions.

Orthopedic manual therapy can help people of all ages experiencing joint pain, muscle tension, limited mobility, or recovering from injury. It's particularly beneficial for athletes, people with chronic pain, post-surgical patients, and anyone with musculoskeletal conditions. Whether you have acute injuries or long-standing issues, our therapists can design a treatment plan tailored to your needs.

We use a variety of evidence-based manual therapy techniques, including joint mobilizations, soft tissue mobilization, myofascial release, therapeutic massage, and stretching. The specific techniques used depend on your condition and what our evaluation reveals. Some patients may need gentle techniques, while others benefit from more intensive manual work. We always customize the approach to your comfort level and tolerance.

Every patient receives a thorough initial evaluation that includes a detailed health history, movement assessment, and special tests to identify the root cause of your problem. Based on this comprehensive assessment, we develop a personalized treatment plan that combines manual therapy techniques with exercises specific to your condition and goals. We regularly assess your progress and adjust your plan as needed to ensure the best outcomes.
