Acupuncture

How does Cha Physical Therapy approach acupuncture?

At Cha Physical Therapy, we integrate acupuncture as a complementary, evidence-based therapy within our holistic Cha Method. This ancient practice, rooted in Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM), uses fine needles to stimulate specific points on the body, promoting natural healing, pain relief, and balance. Combined with our science-backed approaches like breathwork and postural re-education, acupuncture enhances recovery without surgery or medications, helping you achieve optimal movement and wellness.

Conditions We Treat with Acupuncture:

  • Chronic pain: Such as back, neck, shoulder, or joint discomfort from musculoskeletal imbalances
  • Neurological disorders: Supporting conditions like migraines, neuropathy, or post-stroke recovery
  • Movement and mobility challenges: Easing stiffness, muscle tension, and restricted range
  • Stress and wellness: Alleviating anxiety, fatigue, and sleep issues

Common Goals of Wellness & Prevention Programs:

  • Improve balance, coordination, and stability
  • Enhance core strength and posture
  • Prevent chronic pain and overuse injuries
  • Increase energy and movement efficiency
  • Support healthy aging and long-term function
  • Build body awareness and confidence

Our Approach

We tailor each session to your unique needs through a thorough assessment. Sessions are one-on-one, typically lasting 45–60 minutes, in our calming Midtown Manhattan clinic.

Key techniques include:

  • Traditional needle insertion – Gentle, sterile needles placed at acupoints to restore energy flow (Qi) and trigger endorphin release
  • Electro-acupuncture – Adding mild electrical stimulation for deeper muscle relaxation
  • Cupping or gua sha integration – When appropriate, to complement needle therapy
  • Holistic fusion – Blended with CHA's neuromuscular re-education, therapeutic exercises, and education

Frequently Asked Questions about Acupuncture:

Acupuncture at Cha Physical Therapy is an evidence-based complementary therapy that uses sterile, fine needles to stimulate specific acupoints on the body. Based on Traditional Chinese Medicine principles, it promotes natural healing, enhances circulation, reduces pain, and supports overall wellness. We integrate it with our science-backed physical therapy methods like breathwork, postural correction, and therapeutic exercise to create a comprehensive treatment plan tailored to your needs.

Acupuncture at Cha Physical Therapy addresses a wide range of conditions, including:

  • Chronic pain (back, neck, shoulder, joint discomfort)
  • Neurological disorders (migraines, neuropathy, post-stroke recovery)
  • Movement and mobility challenges (stiffness, muscle tension, restricted range)
  • Stress-related issues (anxiety, fatigue, sleep disturbances)

Our practitioners work closely with you to determine if acupuncture is right for your specific situation and how it fits into your overall wellness plan.

Our sessions incorporate several techniques based on your individual needs:

  • Traditional needle insertion: Gentle, sterile needles placed at acupoints to restore energy flow and trigger natural healing responses
  • Electro-acupuncture: Mild electrical stimulation combined with needles for deeper muscle relaxation
  • Cupping or gua sha: Complementary techniques used when appropriate to enhance therapy
  • Holistic fusion: Acupuncture combined with neuromuscular re-education, therapeutic exercises, breathwork, and patient education
