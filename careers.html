Join the team that's elevating physical therapy

At Cha Physical Therapy, we're redefining rehabilitation in Midtown Manhattan by combining evidence-based techniques with compassionate, personalized care. Our close-knit team of physical therapists, therapy assistants, and patient coordinators believes in hands-on treatment, individualized plans, and helping every patient move better, feel better, and live better. We're creating a workplace where clinicians and staff feel supported, challenged, and proud of the lasting impact they make. Whether you're a licensed physical therapist, a skilled assistant, or passionate about patient experience, there's a place for you at Cha.

A rewarding place to grow and make a difference

Cha Physical Therapy is a boutique clinic dedicated to exceptional outcomes in a tranquil, state-of-the-art space. We focus on quality over quantity, with fully private 1-on-1 sessions and a refined environment designed for healing.

  • One premier location in the heart of Midtown Manhattan
  • Expert team delivering faster recovery and performance enhancement
  • Specialized programs in orthopedic manual therapy, Schroth 3D scoliosis treatment, neurological rehabilitation, TMJ relief, and wellness prevention
  • Hundreds of patients returning to pain-free, active lives

Every treatment room and shared space at Cha is crafted to feel calm, professional, and welcoming. Our roles are designed to let you focus on what matters most: delivering undivided attention and transformative care.

Why work at Cha Physical Therapy?

Working at Cha means being part of a practice that truly puts people first—patients and team members alike. From our serene clinic to transparent collaboration, we prioritize an environment where exceptional care begins with an exceptional team. Whether you're clinical or administrative, you'll have the resources, mentorship, and flexibility to advance your career while making a meaningful difference.

Supportive, collaborative culture

  • Small-team dynamic with open communication and genuine mentorship
  • Regular team meetings, case reviews, and professional development opportunities
  • A warm, inclusive environment that values work-life balance

State-of-the-art tools & space

  • Modern equipment for manual therapy, neuromuscular re-education, postural restoration, and specialized techniques
  • Beautiful, tranquil clinic designed as a sanctuary for focused healing

Growth opportunities

  • Ongoing training in advanced certifications (Schroth, manual therapy, etc.)
  • Clear pathways to senior therapist, clinical lead, or specialized roles
  • Support for pursuing passions in orthopedics, neurology, performing arts medicine, or wellness

Look forward to coming to work

  • Treat patients in private, fully equipped rooms with natural light and calm ambiance
  • Collaborate with a passionate, expert team in a refined Midtown space
  • Recharge in dedicated staff areas with complimentary snacks and beverages
  • Thrive in a caring, results-driven culture that celebrates every patient milestone
