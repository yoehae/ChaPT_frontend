Frequently Asked Questions about Cha Physical Therapy:

What is Cha Physical Therapy?

Cha Physical Therapy is a premier physical therapy clinic located in Midtown Manhattan, offering personalized, one-on-one care to help you recover from injuries, manage pain, and improve your overall physical health.

What treatments do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of treatments including manual therapy, therapeutic exercises, dry needling, cupping, electrical stimulation, ultrasound therapy, and customized rehabilitation programs.

What happens during a session?

Each session begins with an assessment of your current condition, followed by hands-on treatment techniques and targeted exercises. Your therapist will work with you one-on-one throughout the entire session.

Where are we located and what are our hours?

We are located at 16 West 32nd Street, Suite 1007, NYC 10001. Our hours are Monday through Friday 9AM-7PM, Saturday 9AM-3:30PM, and we are closed on Sunday.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment by calling us at (212) 643-9326, texting us at 718-506-6239, or using our online booking system.

Do you accept my insurance?

We accept most major insurance plans including Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield, Blue Cross of California, Fidelis Care, United Healthcare, Cigna, and Aetna. Contact us to verify your specific coverage.
